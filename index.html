FAQ

Who receives the mined Bitcoin?
Mining rewards are paid directly by the mining pool you configure to your personal Bitcoin wallet.
Do you hold customer Bitcoin?
No. Nordhash Oy never receives, stores or controls customer Bitcoin.
Can I use my own mining pool?
Yes — customers configure their own pool and receive rewards directly.
When does mining start?
Mining typically starts within minutes of payment once we've allocated a unit and you've provided pool details.
Can I renew my rental?
Yes — renewals are available subject to availability. Use the same payment flow to start a new rental period.
Can I cancel?
Cancellation policies are outlined in the Refund Policy; refunds are prorated for unused rental time if applicable.
Which mining pool should I use?
You can choose any SHA-256 compatible pool. Popular pools usually provide setup guides — choose one you trust.
How long until my miner is online?
Most miners are online within minutes after configuration; in rare cases allocation/setup may take longer.
What if the Internet connection fails?
We monitor connections 24/7. If the connection fails we will attempt to restore service and may offer prorated refunds according to the Refund Policy.
